OUR ENEMIES ARE MANY—

OUR PASSES ARE MORE.

THEY CAME WITH FIRE—

WE DANCED ON THEIR ASHES!

ENGLAND ROARED—

WE SILENCED THE CROWN!

GERMANY CHARGED—

WE PASSED THEM TO DEATH!

ITALY PRAYED—

WE BROKE THEIR DEFENSE!

FRANCE DREAMED—

WE SHATTERED THEIR SLEEP!

SAY IT! SAY IT!

WE ARE BARÇA!

WE ARE MORE THAN A CLUB!

WE ARE THE STORM YOU FEAR AT NIGHT!

WE ARE THE PASS BEFORE THE GOAL!
